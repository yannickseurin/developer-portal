# Companion Wallet

A companion wallet is your own app that is fully compatible with your Device App and will allow your users to make transactions on your chain. 

Requirements:

- The wallet gives an option to verify the receiving address on a Ledger device. 
- The wallet has an affiliate link next to the "Connect with Ledger" option.
- The wallet has GUI running on Windows/MacOS/Linux
